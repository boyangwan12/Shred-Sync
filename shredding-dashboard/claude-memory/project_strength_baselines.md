## Programming notes

- **CNS-limited days (HRV <80):** keep loads, but front-load compounds. Don't let triceps/secondary compounds end the session — they regress. Apr 25's triceps regression (50 × 6 vs 50 × 8 Apr 17) was an order-of-operations effect, not a strength loss.
- **CNS-fresh days (HRV >120):** attempt next-progression weights on top set, especially Bench (一边75), Shoulder Press (一边55), DB Incline (50).
- **Accessories handle low HRV well:** crunch, lateral raise, fly all hit PRs on Apr 25 despite HRV 71.

## When to recalibrate again

- After a deload week (CNS resets — may unlock higher numbers)
- After bodyweight drops below 150 lb (relative strength changes)
- After any 2-session regression at the same weight (suggests need to drop 5–10%)
